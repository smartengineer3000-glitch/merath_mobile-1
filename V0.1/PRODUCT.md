# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Muslim individuals calculating family inheritance for personal estate planning. The user is someone who needs accurate, Islamic-law-compliant estate distribution — whether preparing a will, settling an estate, or understanding their share under Sharia. Context: mobile, often emotional or time-sensitive (bereavement), requiring clarity, trust, and privacy.

## Product Purpose

Merath is a comprehensive Islamic inheritance calculator that accurately computes estate distribution according to the four major Sunni schools of jurisprudence (Hanafi, Maliki, Shafi'i, Hanbali). It transforms classical Fiqh rules into an accessible, modern tool — eliminating manual calculation errors while preserving the full depth of jurisprudential nuance. Success means users can trust the result enough to act on it.

## Brand Personality

Scholarly, Modern, Authoritative. The app bridges centuries of Islamic jurisprudence with precise, modern software — it should feel like consulting a learned scholar who happens to be exceptionally good with numbers.

## Anti-references

- Generic/toy calculator apps (flat, zero personality, no cultural context)
- Overly decorative religious apps (ornamental patterns, low-contrast text, crowded layouts)
- The AI-generated SaaS template (side-stripe borders, gradient text, numbered section markers, tiny uppercase tracked eyebrows)
- Design the interface from the engine's functionality and domain logic outward — not from assumptions about "what Islamic apps look like"

## Design Principles

1. **Accuracy as interface.** Every pixel communicates precision and trust. The calculation engine is the core; the UI is its faithful translator.
2. **Respect for the domain.** Islamic inheritance law is exacting and sacred. The interface treats the subject with appropriate gravity — clear, unhurried, well-spaced.
3. **Complexity hidden, not lost.** The full depth of madhhab-specific rules, awl, radd, and hijab is preserved but surfaced progressively. The default path is simple; the expert path is thorough.
4. **Private by default.** All calculations are local. No data leaves the device. The UI signals this privacy at moments of user trust (before calculation, before export).
5. **Cultural fluency.** Full RTL support, Arabic-first typography, and color that draws from Islamic visual heritage without relying on cliché.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AA compliance for color contrast
- Full RTL and multi-language support (Arabic, English, Urdu, Turkish, French, German)
- Screen reader compatibility
- Minimum 44dp touch targets
- Reduced motion support
