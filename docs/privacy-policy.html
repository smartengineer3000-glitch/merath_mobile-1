Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes how Merath Islamic Inheritance Calculator ("Application," "we," "us," "our," or "Company") collects, uses, and shares information through our mobile application available on Google Play Store.

1. Introduction

Merath Islamic Inheritance Calculator is an educational mobile application designed to assist users in understanding Islamic inheritance distribution according to the principles of Islamic Law (Sharia). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience.

Important: This application is for informational and educational purposes only. It does not provide legal or religious advice. Users should consult with qualified Islamic scholars or legal professionals before making any inheritance-related decisions.

2. What Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you use the Application, we collect information you voluntarily enter:

  • Estate Information: Total estate value, funeral expenses, debts, and wills
  • Heir Information: Number and types of heirs (family relationships)
  • Preference Settings: Selected Islamic school (madhab) - Hanafi, Maliki, Shafi'i, or Hanbali
  • Calculation History: Records of previous calculations stored locally on your device

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

The Application may automatically collect:

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version
  • Usage Information: Which features you use, calculation frequency
  • Crash Reports: App crashes and errors (via Firebase Crashlytics)
  • Analytics Data: App usage patterns to improve features

2.3 Information NOT Collected

We explicitly do NOT collect:

  • Your name, email, or phone number (unless you contact support)
  • Location data or GPS information
  • Biometric data
  • Payment information (app is free)
  • Social media account information

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To Provide Services: Perform calculations based on data you enter
  • To Maintain History: Save and organize your calculation history locally
  • To Improve the App: Understand which features are most used and fix bugs
  • For Analytics: Analyze usage patterns to improve user experience
  • For Safety: Monitor for crashes and technical issues via Firebase
  • To Comply with Law: If required by applicable law or court order

4. Data Storage & Security

4.1 Local Storage

All of your calculation data is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit your calculation data to external servers unless you explicitly choose to export or share it.

4.2 Firebase Services

We use the following Firebase services:

  • Firebase Crashlytics: Automatically reports app crashes to help us fix problems
  • Firebase Analytics: Collects anonymous usage data to understand how the app is used

4.3 Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures including:

  • Encryption of data in transit
  • Secure API communication
  • Regular security updates

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. Please use the Application at your own risk.

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and similar regulations, we collect the following categories of data:

Legal Basis: Our processing of personal data is based on:

  • Legitimate Interest: To improve app functionality and fix bugs
  • User Consent: To process crash reports and analytics

7.1 Your Rights (EU Users)

If you are located in the EU, you have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Restrict processing

7.2 Data Retention (EU)

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide the service or as required by law. Crash and analytics data is retained for up to 90 days.

8. Children's Privacy

The Application is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information and terminate the child's account.

9. Your Privacy Rights

9.1 Access & Deletion

You can:

  • Access your data: All your calculation history is stored on your device and visible in the app
  • Delete your data: Use the "Clear All" function in the Settings to delete all local data

9.2 Opt-Out Options

To opt-out of Firebase analytics:

  • Go to App Settings
  • Toggle "Analytics" to OFF
