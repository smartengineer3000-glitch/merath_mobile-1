FULLY OFFLINE — ZERO DATA COLLECTION
Last updated: July 23, 2026

Overview

Merath ("the App") is a professional Islamic inheritance calculator that operates entirely on your device. We have designed it with your privacy as a foundational principle. This policy explains what we do — and importantly, what we do not do — with your data.

The App makes zero network requests. No data ever leaves your device. There are no servers, no analytics, no tracking, and no accounts.

Data We Do NOT Collect

The App does not collect, transmit, store on remote servers, or share any of the following:

  • Personal information (names, ages, or relationships of heirs)
  • Estate or financial data (asset values, debts, funeral expenses)
  • Calculation results or history
  • Device identifiers or usage analytics
  • Location data
  • Any other information whatsoever

Data Storage

All data entered into the App — including estate details, heir information, calculation results, and your preferred settings — is stored exclusively in local device storage (AsyncStorage on your device). This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible to us or any third party
  • Can be deleted at any time by clearing the App's data or uninstalling the App
  • Is not backed up to any cloud service by the App

Permissions

The App requests no sensitive Android permissions. The following permissions are explicitly blocked:

  • Camera / Microphone — not used
  • Location — not used
  • Contacts — not used
  • Storage (read/write) — not used

The only system-level capability used is local file export (sharing calculation results as text or PDF), which is handled entirely by the operating system's share sheet and does not involve any network transmission.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking services. There are no SDKs that transmit data off your device.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any data from children or any other users. As no data is collected at all, COPPA and similar regulations are inherently satisfied.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above and publish the revised policy at this same URL. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
